
# Simple Timestamp camera, photo editing app! 
### Add time to your memorable photo!


#### Features

- Drag and transform the text
- Automatically extract time from your photo
- Choose colors of text, outline and shadow to make the text beautiful
- Choose a time photo was taken or current time to show in photo
- Can change options of year-month-day time format 
- Can save original photo when you take a photo with TARTE app
- Save your photo with timestamp and share it on social media !
